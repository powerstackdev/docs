# Layers

Top-level groupings that cluster functionality together.

## What is a layer?

Layers are a top-level clustering in Power Stack.  They are an integrated clustering of services used to provide a
specific function to a user.  These layers refer to functionality within Power Stack and not

There are three layers that exist Power Stack.

## What are the layers

- Presentation layer
- Experience layer
- Content layer

### Presentation Layer

This is what a website user will see and interact with and how your website is presented.

### Experience Layer

This is responsible for the editing experience and management of your content.

### Content layer

The underlying content source and reporting system.
